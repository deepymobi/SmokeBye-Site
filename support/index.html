About SmokeBye

SmokeBye is a smoke-free progress tracker that helps you monitor time, money saved, cigarettes avoided, and health recovery milestones.

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers for the most common setup and usage questions.

How do I change my quit plan?

Open SmokeBye, go to Settings, and select Smoke settings. From there, you can update your quit date, cigarettes per day, pack price, currency, and cigarettes per pack.

How do notifications work?

SmokeBye can send daily reminders and recovery milestone alerts. You can manage notification preferences in the app's Notifications settings.

Does SmokeBye require an account?

No. SmokeBye currently works without a user account. Your core quit plan and notification settings are stored locally on your device.

Are recovery values medical advice?

No. Recovery progress and life regained estimates are based on public research timelines and are provided for informational purposes only.
