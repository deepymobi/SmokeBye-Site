Overview

SmokeBye is designed as a local smoke-free tracking app. You can create a quit plan, track savings and recovery milestones, and enable reminders without creating an account.

We aim to minimize the data we handle and to keep your core tracking information on your device whenever possible.

Information we handle

Information stored locally on your device

  • Your quit date and time
  • Your smoking baseline, such as cigarettes per day
  • Your pack price, pack size, and selected currency
  • Your notification preferences

Information you may provide voluntarily

If you contact us for support, you may provide your email address and any information you choose to include in your message.

How information is used

Information in SmokeBye is used to:

  • Generate your smoke-free progress and savings calculations
  • Display health recovery milestones and estimates
  • Schedule optional local reminders and milestone alerts
  • Respond to support requests you send to us

SmokeBye does not require a user account to provide its core tracking features.

Storage and retention

Your quit plan and notification preferences are stored locally on your device using on-device app storage.

We do not intentionally upload your quit plan or smoke-free tracking data to our servers as part of the current version of the app.

Notifications

SmokeBye can send optional local notifications, such as daily reminders and recovery milestone alerts. Notification permissions are requested only when you explicitly allow them.

You can turn notifications on or off at any time in the app's notification settings or in your device settings.

Third-party services

SmokeBye may rely on platform providers such as Apple, Google, or cloud hosting providers for app distribution, notifications, and website delivery. Those providers may process information under their own privacy policies.

If future versions of SmokeBye add analytics, account systems, or cloud sync, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Children's privacy

SmokeBye is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how SmokeBye handles data, please contact us at hello@sixtree.net.
